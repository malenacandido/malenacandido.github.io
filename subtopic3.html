The Negative Effects of Technology on Children
Site Author
 National University


Abstract
 This site explains the negative effects of technology on children by breaking the factors down into several categories. The first, being obesity, demonstrates how technology and childhood obesity have a relationship due to the fact that children watch shows while mindless eating. They also spent less time participating in physical activity, which adds to the weight factor. The second is Social Development, which occurs when children spend more time on their devices and less time outdoors, forming connections with other children. Another factor listed is the decreasing attention span of children. A scary statistic shows that the average attention span of children has decreased by 4 seconds in the last few years due to the usage of technology.
